Release notes - kDrive 3.7.6

Bug fixes

  • Fixed synchronization loops due to incorrect resolution of EDIT-DELETE conflicts.
  • Fixed synchronization loops due to null creation dates of folders.
  • Fixed synchronization loops caused by the destruction and the reconstruction of a branch in the sync folder hierarchy.
  • Fixed the update of the list of folders excluded by the user.
  • Fixed synchronization loops caused by incorrect management of modification dates on FAT systems.
  • Fixed incorrect creation dates set after local edit.
  • Fixed actions on files (e.g., make available locally) whose names contain special characters.
  • Fixed truncated logs.
  • Fixed synchronization loops caused by created directories reusing the identifiers of deleted items.
  • Fixed a bug causing file names containing a new line character not to be excluded when requested by the user.
  • Fixed synchronization loops caused by a change of synchronization directory.
  • Display an error when the upload of the logs fails.
